Refund and Cancellation Policy

Thank you for using SnapCut AI.

1. Cancellations

Since we provide digital credits/services that are processed instantly, cancellations are not possible once a transaction is completed and credits are added to your account.

2. Refunds

Refunds are only issued if there is a technical failure from our side where credits were not delivered after a successful payment. In such cases, please contact us within 7 days of the transaction.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days and credited back to the original payment method.
