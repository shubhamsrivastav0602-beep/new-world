Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

At SnapCut AI, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect images you upload solely for the purpose of background removal. We do not store your images permanently on our servers.

2. Data Usage

Your images are processed using secure AI APIs. Once processed, the images are deleted from our temporary storage within 24 hours.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Razorpay for payment processing and Cloudinary for temporary image hosting. These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@snapcut.ai.
